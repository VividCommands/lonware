The Mission

Lonware was born from a simple frustration: elite hardware being held back by default OS settings, bloated configurations, and unoptimized network stacks. We set out to fix every layer of the stack — from registry to router — so your rig finally performs the way it was designed to.

Every optimization we ship is tested under real competitive conditions. Not synthetic benchmarks, not isolated lab scenarios. If it doesn't hold up in ranked matches, it doesn't ship. Our products are built around one goal: giving you every competitive advantage that software can offer.

No bloatware. No vague promises. No hidden fees. Every claim we make is backed by real results and a community that holds us accountable. If something doesn't work for you, we'll make it right.
