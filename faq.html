General

What is Lonware?

+

Lonware is a gaming optimization platform offering PC tweaks, macro automation, ping optimization, console tweaks, and aim enhancement tools. Our products are built for competitive players who want every advantage that software can provide.

Who are Lonware products for?

+

Our products are designed for competitive gamers on PC and console who want to eliminate software-side performance bottlenecks. Whether you're grinding ranked, playing casually, or streaming, our tools are built to be straightforward and effective.

How much of a performance improvement can I expect?

+

Results vary by system and product. PC Optimizations typically produce 30-50% FPS improvements on mid-range systems. Ping Optimizations commonly reduce average latency by 40-60%. Macro.io provides sub-millisecond execution timing. Results on high-end or already-optimized systems may be smaller.

Safety

Is Lonware safe? Will it break my PC?

+

Yes, Lonware products are safe. Our PC optimization guide covers documented Windows settings — registry tweaks, power plan configuration, and background process management. Every change is documented so you know exactly what was modified and how to reverse it.

Will I get banned in competitive games?

+

Our products modify system-level settings and provide legal macro functionality. PC and network optimizations are OS-level configurations that anti-cheat systems do not flag. Macro.io operates within the bounds of what competitive platforms allow — we do not provide aimbots, wallhacks, or any prohibited game modifications.

How do I undo changes from Lonware products?

+

Every Lonware product comes with reversal documentation. For PC optimizations, we provide a step-by-step guide to restore your original settings. Macro.io simply needs to be closed to stop all macro activity. Check the Docs page for full reversal instructions for each product.

Compatibility

What Windows versions are supported?

+

PC Optimizations and Macro.io support Windows 10 (version 1903 or later) and Windows 11. macOS and Linux are not supported for PC software products. Console Tweaks work independently of your PC and are compatible with PS5, Xbox Series X/S, and current-gen consoles.

What games does Lonware work with?

+

Our system-level optimizations benefit all games. Macro.io supports any game that accepts keyboard or controller input. Bloom Reducer works with any game using visual rendering. We have tested extensively with Fortnite, Warzone, Apex Legends, Valorant, and CS2, but our products are not game-specific.

Do I need specific hardware?

+

For Macro.io, the minimum requirement is an Intel Core i5 / AMD Ryzen 5 or better with 8GB RAM. For PC optimization guides and network optimization guides, any Windows PC is compatible.

Billing & Access

How do I receive my product after purchasing?

+

After purchase, you'll receive access instructions via email within minutes. Macro.io provides a download link. Optimization guides are delivered as digital documents. If you don't receive your access within 30 minutes, check your spam folder or contact us through Discord.

Do I get updates included?

+

Yes. All Lonware products include lifetime updates. As Windows updates, games update, or we discover better optimization methods, your purchase is updated automatically. You pay once and the product continues to improve.
